Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Refund Policy explains when you're eligible for a refund on a Page Instant subscription, and how to request one. Page Instant is a brand operated by Sky's Printer. This policy is part of our Terms & Conditions.

1. Free Trial & Before You Pay

We encourage you to use any free trial or free tier available before subscribing to a paid plan, so you can confirm the Service fits your needs. Once you subscribe, the terms below apply.

2. Eligibility for a Refund

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • You were charged due to a verified billing error (such as a duplicate charge).
  • The Service was unavailable to you for a significant, continuous period due to a fault on our side, and we were unable to resolve it.
  • You request a cancellation within 7 days of your first-ever subscription payment, and you have used less than 10% of your plan's message allowance.

3. What's Not Covered

  • Partial refunds for unused messages after the eligibility window in Section 2 has passed.
  • Refunds requested because a Facebook Page was disconnected due to actions taken by Facebook/Meta (such as permission revocation), since this is outside our control.
  • Refunds for accounts suspended or terminated due to a violation of our Terms & Conditions.
  • Renewal charges where the cancellation request was made after the renewal had already processed.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email support@pageinstant.site with your account email and the reason for your request. Include your payment receipt or transaction ID if you have it — this helps us process your request faster.

5. Processing Time

We review refund requests within 3–5 business days of receiving them. If approved, refunds are issued to your original payment method and may take additional time to appear, depending on your bank or payment provider — typically 5–10 business days.

6. Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, or by emailing support. Cancelling stops future renewals but doesn't automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period unless it falls within the eligibility window described in Section 2.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with a new "last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For any refund or billing question, reach us at support@pageinstant.site or through our contact page.
